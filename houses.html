Game of Thrones Houses

Discover the Great Houses of Game of Thrones

The politics in the Game of Thrones series is all about the Great Houses: from the noble and proud Starks to the wealthy and cunning Lannisters. Each family has its own history, heritage, and quest for power. On this page, you'll discover more about each house and their role in the battle for the Iron Throne. Dive into a world of political intrigue, alliances, and betrayal as you explore the tales of the Great Houses.

House Stark

House Stark is the ruling house of the North and its seat is at Winterfell. Their sigil is a grey direwolf on a white field.

  • Location: Winterfell
  • Motto: "Winter Is Coming"
  • Personalities:
    • Eddard Stark
    • Catelyn Stark
    • Sansa Stark
    • Arya Stark
    • Rob Stark
    • Brandon Stark

"Winter Is Coming"

House Lannister

House Lannister is one of the wealthiest and most powerful families in the Seven Kingdoms. Their seat is at Casterly Rock. Their sigil is a golden lion on a crimson field.

  • Location: Casterly Rock
  • Motto: "Hear Me Roar!"
  • Personalities:
    • Tywin Lannister
    • Cersei Lannister

"Hear Me Roar!"

House Baratheon

House Baratheon is the youngest of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms, and its seat is at Storm's End. Its sigil is a black stag on a gold field.

  • Location: Storm's End
  • Motto: "Ours is the Fury"
  • Personalities:
    • Robert Baratheon
    • Stannis Baratheon

"Ours is the fury"

House Targaryen

House Targaryen is a family said to be descended from the Valyrian Freehold, a powerful empire in Essos, who conquered the Seven Kingdoms with three gigantic dragons ridden by Aegon the Conqueror and his two sisters. This followed almost three centuries of Targaryen rule at King's Landing. Their sigil is a red three-headed dragon on a black field.

  • Location: King's Landing
  • Motto: "Fire and Blood"
  • Personalities:
    • Aegon Targaryen
    • Daenerys Targaryen

"Fire and Blood"

House Arryn

House Arryn is one of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms and its seat is at the Eyrie fortress. Its sigil is a white falcon soaring against a blue sky.

  • Location: The Eyrie
  • Motto: "As High as Honor"
  • Personalities:
    • Jon Arryn
    • Lysa Arryn

"As High as Honor"

House Tyrell

House Tyrell is one of the Great Houses of Westeros. Their seat is at Highgarden. Their sigil is a golden rose on a green field.

  • Location: Highgarden
  • Motto: "Growing Strong"
  • Personalities:
    • Mace Tyrell
    • Olenna Tyrell

"Growing Strong"

House Greyjoy

House Greyjoy of Pyke is one of the Great Houses of Westeros. It rules over the Iron Islands, a harsh and bleak collection of forbidding islands off the west coast of Westeros, from the castle of Pyke on the island of the same name. Their sigil is a golden kraken on a black field.

  • Location: Pyke
  • Motto: "We Do Not Sow"
  • Personalities:
    • Balon Greyjoy
    • Yara Greyjoy

"We Do Not Sow"

House Martell

House Martell of Sunspear is one of the Great Houses of Westeros. It rules the peninsula of Dorne in the far south of the continent from their castle Sunspear. Their sigil is a red sun pierced by a gold spear.

  • Location: Sunspear
  • Motto: "Unbowed, Unbent, Unbroken"
  • Personalities:
    • Oberyn Martell
    • Ellaria Sand

"Unbowed, Unbent, Unbroken"

House Tully

House Tully of Riverrun is one of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms. It is the principal house in the Riverlands, and its seat is at Riverrun. Their sigil is a silver trout leaping on a blue and red striped field.

  • Location: Riverrun
  • Motto: "Family, Duty, Honor"
  • Personalities:
    • Hoster Tully
    • Catelyn Stark

"Family, Duty, Honor"
